Trail Planning Dashboard

Video walkthrough of the dashboard

This project was created to assist Trempealeau County, Wisconsin in beginning to plan out trail development. The dashboard was built in Experience Builder and utilizes a custom suitability basemap to identify areas most suitable for trail development based on ground slope and parcel ownership. It has two main pieces of functionality - users can draw their own trail and receive elevation statistics, and they also find information about parcels the drawn line intersects. With this information stakeholders can better move forward with a potential trail for development and land easement.

Technologies Used

-- ArcGIS Pro
-- Raster analysis
-- Experience Builder
